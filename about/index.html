Hi there, I'm an astroparticle physics Ph.D. student at Rice University in Houston, TX. I'm always looking for new ways to bridge machine learning with particle physics. Currently I'm working on autoencoders to reconstruct the energy of particle interactions measured in XENONnT, a direct detection dark matter experiment based in the Laboratori Nazionali del Gran Sasso in Italy.
